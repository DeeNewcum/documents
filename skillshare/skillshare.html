North Branch Projects north branch projects.com Albany Park bookbinding
Open six days a week to teach bookbinding. Make a book for them, then make a book for yourself.
FreeGeek Chicago freegeek chicago.org Logan Square computer repair
Teaches repair and recycling of computer hardware, and Linux installation. Build a computer for them, then build a computer for yourself.
West Town Bikes west town bikes.org West Town bicycle repair
Provides tools and equipment and knowledge to repair bicycles.
Pumping Station: One pumping station one.org Avondale hackerspace
For $40/month, members get access to 3D printers, a laser cutter, a woodshop, a metal shop, electrical engineering equipment, sewing machines and looms, and fellow geeks who can help learn how to use them.
South Side Hub south side hub.org Hyde Park woodwork
A cultural community space. Provides a woodshop for use by the public.
