North Branch Projects north branch projects.com Albany Park bookbinding
Teaches bookbinding. Make a book for them, then make a book for yourself. Open shop for 24 hours a week.
FreeGeek Chicago freegeek chicago.org Logan Square computer refurbishing
Teaches computer hardware refurbishing and Linux installation. Build a computer for them, then build a computer for yourself. Open shop for 12 hours a week.

Provides tools and equipment to repair bicycles. Open shop for 9 hours a week.
Pumping Station: One pumping station one.org Avondale hackerspace
For $40/month, members get access to 3D printers, a laser cutter, a woodshop, a metal shop, electrical engineering equipment, and sewing machines and looms.
South Side Hub south side hub.org Hyde Park woodwork
A cultural community space. Provides a woodshop for use by the public. Open shop for 2 hours a week.

Monthly skill-share sessions on a variety of topics.
